(yet) Bio

Dr. Sungil Kim is an Associate Professor in the Department of Industrial Engineering at UNIST. He is dedicated to tackling modern challenges in quality engineering within the ongoing era of AI transformation—including sensor drift, inefficient data representation, and class imbalance. His work leverages sensor data and real-time log data to provide practical, field-ready solutions.
